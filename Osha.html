OSHA Construction Training

At SafetyNet's OSHA Training Center, we are committed to providing comprehensive training programs that meet the standards set by the Occupational Safety and Health Administration (OSHA).

Course Module

  • History and Mission of OSHA
  • OSHA Standards and Regulations

  • Employer Responsibilities
  • Employee Rights and Responsibilities

  • OSHA Inspection Process
  • Responding to OSHA Inspections

Description

Welcome to the "Introduction to OSHA" course. This course provides an overview of the Occupational Safety and Health Administration, its mission, and the critical role it plays in maintaining workplace safety standards. This introductory module is essential for anyone looking to understand the fundamental principles of OSHA and its impact on workplace safety.

What You Will Learn

  • The history and purpose of OSHA and its impact on workplace safety.
  • Employer and employee rights and responsibilities under OSHA regulations.
  • How OSHA inspections are conducted and how to prepare for them.
